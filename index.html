Privacy Policy for Summary AI

This Privacy Policy explains how Summary AI ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and handles your information when you use our mobile application.

Last Updated: February 5, 2026

1. Introduction

Summary AI ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We aim to collect the minimum amount of data necessary to provide our services.

  • Authentication: We use Google Sign-In and Apple Sign-In. We do not store personal details beyond the default parameters provided by Firebase Authentication (such as your UID, email address, and name).
  • User Content: You may upload documents for processing. These documents are stored and processed on our secure servers solely to provide the summary service.
  • Subscription Data: We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect transaction receipts and basic device identifiers to ensure your premium features are active across devices.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account.
  • To process and summarize the documents you upload.
  • To manage your subscriptions and in-app purchases.
  • Analytics: We use internal analytics to track app usage and performance. This data is used solely to improve the app and is never shared with third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your documents are stored securely. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information or uploaded content to any third party for marketing or any other purpose.

5. User Control: Deletion and Access

We believe in full user autonomy.

  • Document Deletion: You have the freedom to delete any uploaded document at any time within the app. Once deleted, it is removed from our processing systems.
  • Account Deletion: You may delete your account and all associated personal data at any time via the settings menu in the app. This action is permanent and satisfies Apple's mandatory account deletion requirements.

6. Third-Party Services

While we do not share data for marketing, we use the following trusted providers to run the app:

  • Google/Apple: For secure authentication.
  • Firebase: For backend infrastructure and authentication.
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management and receipt validation.

7. Children's Privacy

Summary AI does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us for immediate deletion.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

9. Contact Us
